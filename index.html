Tips and Tricks

Will your A/C make it through Summer?

Air conditioning is very useful in summer as it helps to keep indoor spaces cool and comfortable, which can improve productivity, sleep quality, and overall well-being. It can also help to reduce the risk of heat-related illnesses and improve air quality by filtering out pollutants and allergens.

Preparing Your Home Before Vacation

Before going on vacation, it is recommended to prepare your home for extended periods without using the air conditioning system. This can include adjusting the thermostat settings, cleaning or replacing air filters, and closing blinds or curtains to reduce heat gain. Additionally, it's important to ensure that the AC unit is in good condition and schedule any necessary maintenance or repairs before leaving.

Is a More Expensive HVAC Filter Better?

More expensive HVAC filters may offer better filtration and capture smaller particles, but their effectiveness depends on the specific needs and environment of the home. It's important to choose a filter with the appropriate MERV rating and replace it regularly to maintain good air quality and maximize the lifespan of the HVAC system.
